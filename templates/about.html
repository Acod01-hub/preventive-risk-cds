About the Preventive Risk & Clinical Decision Support System

A demonstration prototype showcasing the integration of machine learning and generative AI in clinical decision support systems.

Purpose & Vision

This prototype demonstrates how advanced AI technologies can be integrated into healthcare workflows to improve patient outcomes through:

  • Early risk identification and intervention
  • Personalized treatment recommendations
  • Evidence-based clinical decision support
  • Improved patient-provider communication

Current Implementation

The current prototype focuses on diabetes risk assessment using established clinical parameters:

  • Age: Known risk factor for diabetes
  • BMI: Body mass index correlation with metabolic health
  • Blood Pressure: Hypertension as diabetes risk factor
  • Blood Sugar: Fasting glucose levels
  • Lifestyle: Physical activity impact on risk
